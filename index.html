    Survey Subjects & Methods

    Using the Ibaraki Prefecture Medical Function Information System, we conducted a survey of 36 medical institutions in Tsukuba City that offer "language-free medical care." (Permission to use information from this site has been obtained.)
    The survey was conducted around October 2023.
    (Citation: https://www.pref.ibaraki.jp/soshiki/hokenfukushi/koso/index.html)
    (Survey Contents：https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1GDuiCWaFqcSmz4HnfbI4ndq8yn-GFevT-bs1ieEYy7M/edit)

    Survey Limitations

    The accuracy of the information cannot be guaranteed due to limited survey methods.
    For example, as time passes, staff who can speak foreign languages ​​may be transferred, or may not be available when you visit for some reason.
